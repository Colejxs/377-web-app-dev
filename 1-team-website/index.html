Welcome to the Hanover Hawks

The Hanover Football Huddle Club’s mission is to provide our student-athletes at Hanover High School with the best possible football experience. As a “football family”, our club members actively provide community support, promote school spirit, and encourage team comradery by raising funds, sponsoring events, and when funds are available providing academic scholarships. We provide any other assistance to the football program as needed and in accordance with the established bylaws of the organization.

Objectives and Purpose

  • Encourage the growth and development of our football program at Hanover High School
  • Provide financial support for projects which promote the football program that fall within the policies and guidelines of Hanover High School, but which are not funded by the school district.
  • Provide financial support that encourages the growth and development of programs related to the football program. All support will fall within the policies and guidelines of Hanover High School.
  • Support the continued growth and success of Hanover Athletics by maintaining clear communications and cooperation between HFHC, the school administration, coaches, players.
  • Disseminate information on programs and projects that will attract and involve a large, active group of parents of football players. Cooperate with the HHS administration, school officials and coaches in the pursuit of these objectives.

Support Provided

  • Coaching Tools — Supplemental teaching tools beyond the ability of the Athletic Department to purchase beneficial to coaching student-athletes is funded, such as HUDL (video analysis tool) subscriptions;
  • Equipment The HFHC has purchased additional essential equipment that falls outside the ability of the HHS Athletic Department to fund. Recent purchases included helping pay for the new game uniforms, video/audio equipment, and a variety of incidental equipment needs falling beyond the team budget;
  • Senior Medal — Senior Medals are purchased for each student-athlete who graduates with a varsity letter in football;
  • Scholarships — scholarships are being looked into for next year for worthy college bound student-athletes; provided funds are available.
  • Meals/ Snacks — Meals are provided to student-athletes participating in varsity away games. Nutritious energy snacks, such as fresh oranges and/or bananas, are provided to student-athletes during half-time;
  • Awards — Awards are purchased to enable recognition to student-athletes for outstanding accomplishments;
  • Year End Banquet — A year-end celebratory banquet is organized and funded, inclusive of decorations, meals, and senior gifts.
